Engagement Flow

A practical security assessment process.

Every project benefits from a simple and repeatable structure: understand scope, simulate realistic attacker behavior, document evidence, then deliver remediation guidance that teams can execute.

01

Scoping

Define targets, rules of engagement, visibility level, test depth, and whether the assessment is white box or black box.

02

Assessment

Perform structured testing across attack surfaces, chaining findings where needed to reflect realistic exploitation paths.

03

Reporting

Document findings with impact, technical details, proof of concept notes, and prioritized remediation recommendations.

04

Follow-Through

Support validation, retesting, and clarification so remediation is measurable and aligned with your operational needs.
